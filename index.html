Best with a MIDI keyboard

Connect a digital piano by USB and open the site in Chrome or Edge — every note you play is checked in real time. No keyboard? You can still learn with the on-screen piano.

How each lesson works

For every exercise: ① Learn — watch and listen. ② Practice — press Start and play; the app waits for you. ③ Test — one count-in measure, then play in tempo. Use any order and skip freely, in every key your instructor assigns.

When you're signed in with your class codes, your completed exercises, practice counts, and Test results are reported to your instructor automatically.
